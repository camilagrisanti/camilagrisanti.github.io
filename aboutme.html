About Me, Camila Grisanti
I am fourth-year undergraduate student at Northwestern Universiy studying Neuroscience with a concentration in Human Behavior and Cognition and Computer Science at the Weinberg college of Arts and Sciences. I work as a peer mentor and teaching assistant for an introductory Python course, helping to lead discussion sections and grade homeworks, quizzes and projects. I intend to start my career as a Software Engineer, and am particularly interested in user experience with different interfaces. Because of this, I have done plenty of work involving web development and design to improve readability and maximize user experience.
